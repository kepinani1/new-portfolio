About Me

Ipek graduated from The Wharton School-University of Pennsylvania in May 2016 where she received a Bachelor of Science Degree in Finance and Real Estate. Upon graduation, she started working at KPMG LLP in August of 2016 as an Associate within the Operations and Compliance Risk group, New York, NY office. She has worked on several client engagements with an emphasis on financial services, and risk management.

Although her experience is in financial services, she has always had an interest in how technology can be leveraged to develop innovative solutions for businesses, and help automate processes. She has coding skills in Excel VBA, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Ipek has worked on various front end projects. Some of her work include developing a political search browser, crystals collector, and animated gif game. For more information, please refer to the Github deployed links in the header above.
